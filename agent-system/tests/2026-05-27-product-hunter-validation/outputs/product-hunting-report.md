## Shortlist

Primary: **P2 Extendable Floor Phone Tripod**

Reason:

- Best fit for one-person filming and shaky-video fix angles.
- Highest review count in provided synthetic data.
- Useful content potential: setup, framing, solo filming, before/after stability.
- Commission is stronger than P1/P4 in provided data.

Secondary: **P1 Mini Desk Phone Tripod**

Reason:

- Safer low-price beginner desk setup angle.
- Lower risk than P3/P4.
- Good for “small desk/góc quay tại nhà” content.

Hold: **P3 Ring Light Tripod Kit**

Reason:

- Good content potential and commission, but higher claim risk and lower provided review confidence.
- Needs live/spec validation before claims around light quality.

Avoid as primary: **P4 Ultra-cheap Phone Stand**

Reason:

- Weak fit for tripod/shaky-video problem.
- Low provided review count and no height adjustment.
