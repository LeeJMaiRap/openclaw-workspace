# Business Handoff Simulation Input

## Scenario

Plan a safe draft affiliate campaign for budget phone tripod.

## Team Available

- Business PM Agent
- Content Copy Agent
- Performance Analyst Agent

## Constraints

- Verification Level: Paper.
- Draft/report only.
- No posting.
- No customer messaging.
- No ads.
- No purchases/orders.
- No payment/API-key usage.
- No fake personal experience.
- Human approval gate required before external action.
