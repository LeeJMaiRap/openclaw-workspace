# Affiliate Strategy Brief: Budget Phone Tripod Campaign

## Strategic Question

Which channel/angle should a one-person affiliate operator test first for budget phone tripod promotion?

## Current State

- Product category: budget phone tripod.
- Audience: Vietnamese beginner content creators.
- Channels available: TikTok, Facebook, Shopee affiliate.
- Evidence: scenario assumptions only; no live market data in this test.

## Goal

Create safe 2-week draft campaign plan that can later feed copywriting and performance analysis.

## Constraints / Non-goals

- Draft only.
- No auto-posting.
- No customer messaging.
- No ads.
- No payment/API-key usage.
- No fake personal experience.
- No guaranteed sales/income claims.

## Audience

Vietnamese beginner creators who want stable video with low budget and minimal setup.

## Options

| Option | Pros | Cons | Risk | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| TikTok problem/solution | visual, easy demo, strong pain point | needs consistent short videos | Medium | best fit for shaky-video angle |
| Facebook beginner guide | trust/context, checklist-friendly | lower organic reach possible | Low | good for explanation and comments |
| Shopee deal-first | direct buying intent | can sound spammy, weak trust | Medium | use only with clear voucher/value evidence |

## Decision Matrix

| Criteria | Weight | TikTok problem/solution | Facebook beginner guide | Shopee deal-first |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Audience pain | 20% | 5 | 4 | 3 |
| Content fit | 20% | 5 | 4 | 3 |
| Trust/risk | 20% | 3 | 4 | 2 |
| Execution ease | 20% | 4 | 4 | 3 |
| Evidence confidence | 20% | 3 | 3 | 2 |
| Total | 100% | 4.0 | 3.8 | 2.6 |

## Recommendation

Start with TikTok problem/solution as primary channel and Facebook beginner guide as support channel. Avoid deal-first positioning until there is strong voucher/price evidence.

## Positioning

"Stable video setup for beginner creators without expensive gear."

## Content Pillars

1. Shaky video problem.
2. Beginner setup checklist.
3. One-person filming tips.
4. Tripod buying checklist.
5. Before/after framing examples.

## Campaign Roadmap

| Week | Focus | Outputs | Success Metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | validate pain/angle | 3 TikTok drafts + 1 Facebook guide draft | CTR target >= 3% after posting approval |
| 2 | iterate winners | 2 variants of best hook + 1 comparison/checklist | CTR and order conversion trend |

## Experiment Backlog

| Experiment | Hypothesis | Metric | Success Threshold |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shaky before/after hook | visible problem improves clicks | CTR | >= 4% |
| Beginner setup checklist | checklist improves saves/comments | engagement rate | baseline +20% |
| Deal-first caption | voucher drives clicks only if offer strong | CTR + CVR | >= current best |

## Metrics Plan

Track:

- views
- clicks
- CTR
- orders
- conversion rate
- commission
- refund/cancel if available
- content angle
- platform

## Risks / Compliance Notes

- Do not claim personal use unless true.
- Do not claim product is best without comparison evidence.
- Do not promise sales/content success.
- Mark draft scripts as draft until human approves.

## Approval Gates

Human approval required before:

- posting content;
- messaging users;
- running ads;
- changing affiliate links;
- using API/payment keys;
- making personal-experience claims.

## Handoffs

- Market Research Agent: validate competitor/content trend and audience pain.
- Product Hunter Agent: compare tripod variants, price, reviews, commission, risks.
- Content Copy Agent: create hooks/captions/video scripts from pillars.
- Performance Analyst Agent: analyze post metrics after approved posting.

## Open Questions

1. Which exact tripod/product link?
2. Is personal usage/footage available?
3. Is TikTok posting approved after draft review?
