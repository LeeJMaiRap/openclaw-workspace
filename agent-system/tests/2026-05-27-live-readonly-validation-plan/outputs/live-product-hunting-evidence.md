## Product Decision

### Primary remains: P2-style extendable floor phone tripod

Reason:

- Live public spec evidence confirms existence of 50-inch extendable phone tripods with remote and phone holder.
- Evidence matches prior Paper shortlist needs: height, remote, phone clamp, solo filming/vlogging/livestream fit.
- Safer than ring-light kit because claims can stay around physical positioning/framing, not lighting/performance.

### Secondary remains: P1-style mini/desk tripod

Reason:

- Public article evidence supports tabletop/compact tripod use cases.
- But live validation here found stronger direct P2-style evidence than P1 SKU evidence.

### Hold remains: P3 ring-light tripod kit

Reason:

- Some public article evidence mentions LED fill light products, but quality, safety, durability, lighting effect remain unverified.

### Avoid primary remains: P4 ultra-cheap phone stand

Reason:

- No live evidence found supporting it as a strong shaky-video / one-person filming solution.

## Allowed Product Claims After This Evidence

Allowed if worded cautiously:

- `Dòng tripod điện thoại dạng kéo dài 50 inch có tồn tại, thường đi kèm kẹp điện thoại và remote.`
- `Nên kiểm tra chiều cao, độ mở kẹp, remote, tải trọng, chất liệu và chân đế trước khi mua.`
- `Tripod giúp cố định điện thoại để khung hình ổn định hơn so với cầm tay trong nhiều tình huống.`
- `Phù hợp để cân nhắc cho quay một mình/livestream nếu thông số sản phẩm đáp ứng nhu cầu.`

Not allowed:

- Product-specific price, voucher, stock.
- `best`, `rẻ nhất`, `bền`, `chắc chắn`, `remote ổn định` as guaranteed claims.
- Review/rating/sales claims.
- Personal testing/use.
- Marketplace seller reliability/authenticity.

## Result

PASS with limits: live public evidence supports P2-style extendable floor phone tripod as primary candidate type. Marketplace proof for price/rating/reviews/sales remains blocked/incomplete due fetch limits and bot walls.
