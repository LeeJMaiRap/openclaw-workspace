## Review Verdict

PASS with required edits.

The copy brief is mostly within evidence boundaries, but several phrases should be softened before publish-ready drafting:

- `kẹp chắc` implies clamp reliability/stability not proven by live evidence.
- `đủ cao` can imply known adequacy for all users; better as `chiều cao phù hợp nhu cầu`.
- `trước tiên` can imply priority recommendation; better as `đáng cân nhắc`.
- `chân đế ổn` implies stability/quality judgment; better as `chân đế phù hợp` or `kiểu chân đế phù hợp`.
- `khả năng gấp gọn` is safe only if listing/spec shows folded size or portability.

No severe violations found: draft does not include price, voucher, rating, review, sales, personal-test, seller-authenticity, or income/viral claims.

## Evidence Boundary

Validated evidence supports:

- Category-level claim: tripod can help hold/fix phone position and improve framing stability compared with handheld shooting.
- Product-type claim: 50-inch extendable phone tripods with holder and remote exist publicly.
- Use-case claim: beginner/casual creator/solo filming/livestream positioning exists in public content.
- Checklist claim: height, clamp, remote, load, material, base/legs, portability should be checked.

Evidence does not support:

- Guaranteed stability.
- Clamp quality or firmness.
- Remote reliability.
- Durability.
- Best/cheapest/current-price claims.
- Platform review/sales/rating claims.
- Personal test/use claims.

## Safer Revised Short Caption

```text
Quay bằng điện thoại mà khung hình hay rung/lệch? Bắt đầu bằng checklist tripod: chiều cao phù hợp, kẹp vừa máy, có remote nếu cần quay một mình, chân đế phù hợp nhu cầu và khả năng gấp gọn nếu anh cần mang theo. Dòng tripod kéo dài khoảng 50 inch là lựa chọn đáng cân nhắc cho quay một mình hoặc livestream — miễn là thông số kẹp, chiều cao và tải trọng phù hợp với nhu cầu của anh.
```

Safety rationale:

- Replaces `đủ cao` with `chiều cao phù hợp`.
- Replaces `chân đế ổn` with `chân đế phù hợp nhu cầu`.
- Qualifies remote and portability by use case.
- Keeps `đáng cân nhắc`, not superiority/performance guarantee.

   - load capacity;
   - folded size/weight if mentioning portability;
   - material/base/leg details if mentioning them.
5. Remove all facts not visible in chosen product source.
6. Recheck price/voucher/stock same day if copy ever includes them; current recommendation remains omit.
7. Run final Claim Safety review on publish-ready draft.
